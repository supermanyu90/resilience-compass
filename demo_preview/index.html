Resilience Compass

An on-device AI copilot for banks' operational resilience — run a live crisis simulation, self-assess against your regulators (ISO 22301, EU DORA, APRA, HKMA…), and triage incidents. Everything is powered by Google Gemma running locally, so it works offline and your data never leaves the device.

Real on-device Gemma via local Ollama. The crisis simulation, coaching and report above are generated by Gemma running on this machine — offline, private. Nothing is sent to a server.
